FAQ

Welcome to the help section. Here are some frequently asked questions:

  • Q: How do I add a product?
    A: Use the 'Add Product' form to input the ASIN and product name.
  • Q: How can I delete a product?
    A: Click the 'Remove' button on the product listing.
  • Q: What is ASIN?
    A: It stands for Amazon Standard Identification Number, unique for each product.
